Ishita Agarwal

Hello! I am Ishita Agarwal. I am 18 and in my second year of BBA at ICFAI Business School, Hyderabad, trying to figure out this whole 'adulting' thing one day at a time.

I grew up watching my dad run his business, and it definitely planted an entrepreneurial seed in my head. But the plot twist? Instead of jumping into the family business, I am out here trying to find my own path. I am eager to learn and maximize my surface area as I figure out what truly excites me.

Life's been pretty interesting - went from being a total science nerd in high school to doing BBA. Quite the switch up, I know! >_<. But that's what growing up is about, taking unexpected turns and seeing where it leads to.

Speaking of turns, I recently wrapped up my first real-world gig as a Customer Relationship Officer at Calvin Klein. Spending 9 hours on your feet isn't exactly a walk in the park, but getting your first paycheck? Total game-changer! Now, I am off to explore the tech world in Bangalore and San Francisco.
